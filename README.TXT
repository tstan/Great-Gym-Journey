PROJECT TITLE: GREAT GYM JOURNEY
VERSION
DATE: 12/8/2013

HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: Hit Run, Use arrow keys to select a choice on screen.

AUTHORS: Theo Tan, Franklin Chang, Jacob Rickman, Jared Speck, Spencer Chang, Joseph Kim

USER INSTRUCTIONS:
Story: You are a person trying to get to the gym to work out. However, many fastfood restaurants are in your way.
Their objective? To prevent you from getting to the gym. DESTROY THEM ALL!!!! MWAHAHAHAHHAHA

Included: Mean fast food restaurants fire mean fast foods. Introduction Level helps to get a player oriented with the controls.
The controls are WASD or arrow keys to move and "space" to fire rocks.
Dodge the foods while destroying enemies on the right.
Once you get to the gym, you win.
